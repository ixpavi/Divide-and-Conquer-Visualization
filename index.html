Introduction to Divide and Conquer

The Divide and Conquer Paradigm

Divide and Conquer is a fundamental algorithmic paradigm based on multi-branched recursion. It works by breaking down a problem into two or more sub-problems of the same or related type, until these become simple enough to be solved directly. The solutions to the sub-problems are then combined to give a solution to the original problem.

The 3 Core Steps

1. Divide

Break the problem into smaller sub-problems that are similar to the original problem.

2. Conquer

Solve the sub-problems recursively. If the sub-problem sizes are small enough (base case), solve them directly.

3. Combine

Combine the solutions of the sub-problems into the solution for the original problem.
